ABOUT US

“Quizzing is not a sport ? Say that to my fast reflex skills, trained brain and toned butt.”

We at the Quiz Club welcome all those with the pathological problem of knowing a lot of stuff, and others too. We aim to introduce the campus community to the joys of quizzing, question framing and if you’ve never answered questions that don’t pertain to academics, don’t worry, we’re here to teach you how to quiz and how to make a quiz too!

We hold quizzes specifically for the freshers, but we also do hold open quizzes, in which anyone from the campus community can join in.We also take part in external competitions and invite your own ideas if you’ve found a competition that we can compete in.
