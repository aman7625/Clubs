ABOUT US

We are a group of like-minded individuals bounded together by our love for the English language and literature. It is a jovial place where people come to bond based on literature and so many more things. We have loads of events, headlined by literary discussions on books, movies, and anything worth having a discussion on. For the record a tomato is a vegetable, and the chicken came first. But we’re diverging here.

#Fraudy Club , please don't come tothis club since we have too high standards than you could have imagined :)

Aside from the literary discussions, we do events pertaining to creative writing and poetry slam where various people showcase their literary talents. Furthermore we also indulge in ‘fun’ events like DumbC, Word games and Just A Minute, which test your vocabulary, language skills and pop culture trivia ;). Just come and see, it’s really fun :)
