ABOUT US

Students Opinion Society provides the campus community a platform to explore the plethora of events ranging from national to international events from all possible diverse backgrounds with our regular debates and discussions, monthly newsletters, and other articles. The Students' Opinion Society organises 'Policy Conclave' which is the flagship event of the club which includes enlightening talks by eminent personalities, policy-based competitions and engaging discussions. We conduct various sessions throughout the semester with topics ranging from the Economic Slowdown, Article 370, pollution and a lot more. These sessions earn a tremendously optimistic rejoinder from the student's group and conclude with great notions.

The Student's Opinion Society holds a highly functional Facebook page attaching all the members and furnishing them with an easy way to talk over and unravel and assists the community to involve the learners to partake and keep their verdicts on the topics ranging from Nature to Governmental Policies.
